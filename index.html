What is the sodium correction calculator?
The sodium correction calculator is a tool that fixes the results of serum sodium [Na⁺] measurements.
How to calculate corrected sodium?
To calculate corrected sodium you should use the following formula:
corrected sodium = patients sodium + 0.024 * (serum glucose - 100)
